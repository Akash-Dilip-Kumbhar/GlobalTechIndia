About i.safe MOBILE GmbH


Global-Tech Partner Since 2020

i.safe MOBILE GmbH has established itself worldwide as a specialist in explosion proof and extremely resilient mobile communication devices and as an innovation leader. They develop and market devices such as mobile phones, smartphones, tablet PCs etc. for safe use in hazardous areas and for robust use in industry and outdoors

i.safe products are precisely tailored to the requirements of our customers in the Oil & Gas, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Mining, and other demanding branches of industry. All i.safe devices are approved with ATEX, IECEX and numerous global standards.
