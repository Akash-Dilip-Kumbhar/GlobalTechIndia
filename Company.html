About US


Global-Tech is one of India’s leading marketing and servicing organization, focused on providing perfect engineering solutions for Imported Machinery to Indian manufacturing industry. Global-Tech has gained a vast experience in a decade, by fulfilling exact needs of manufacturing companies across a wide spectrum.

We have further focused on setting up joint ventures with overseas companies for local manufacturing and services to ensure enhanced support to customers.

Global-Tech is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified engineering organization, employing highly experienced engineers and marketing professionals. We provide our customers what they really deserve and what they really need. This has given us credibility, respect and success around the world. We do not just sell machines; we totally satisfy our customer’s needs, making them a success story.

Global-Tech has undertaken several initiatives through its Business Excellence Model, with the aim of achieving substantial growth and long term perspective. With Global-Tech’s Business Excellence Model, we are consistently focusing on improving internal operational efficiency and satisfaction of important stakeholders like Customers, Principles and Employees.

Global-Tech has a wide sales network covering all over India for easy accessibility to customers. Many of these key locations are also equipped with Service teams to ensure quick response to customers service needs. We have our own warehousing facility at Pune for easy accessibility to our customers.

Our strength is our Associates, who have more than 15 years of International Engineering Experience, fully operational offices and warehousing facility at Europe, UK and USA.

Our Mission


We are unique & leading Global Engineering Solution provider & dynamic Marketing Hub.

By working unitedly as a family with our supportive customers and understanding suppliers, we aim to be Number One in Our Field.
