Technology Leader For Innovative Blower Systems

PILLER is pioneer and world renowned for a wide range of Industrial blowers. PILLER is the technology leading manufacturer of blower systems in process technology. We have the world class know-how for industrial blower systems in the most challenging processes.

Products :

  • Mechanical Vapour Recompression Blowers (MVR)
  • Continuous Catalyst Regeneration Blowers (CCR)
  • Hot Gas circulation fans (Upto 1150 deg.C)
  • Blowers for Surpuric Acid Plant
  • Special Fan Systems designed to customer specifications
